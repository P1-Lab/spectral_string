# Instrument Philosophy

Spectral String is not designed as an interface.

It is designed as a **negotiation between mechanical reality and spectral interpretation**.

---

## Against Interface Thinking

Traditional instruments:
- separate performer and sound engine
- encode gesture as control signal

Spectral String:
- removes the control layer
- collapses gesture into physical causality

The performer does not "control sound".
They **shape conditions under which sound becomes stable**.

---

## The String as Argument

Each string is a claim about:
- tension
- material memory
- excitation response

Playing is the act of disputing that claim.

---

## Stability as Musical Material

In this system:
- instability is not noise
- stability is not silence
- both are compositional variables

---

## No Separation of Hardware and Sound

There is no “instrument” and “synthesizer”.

There is only:
- mechanical excitation
- state resolution
- spectral emergence

---

## Core Principle

> The instrument is not played.
> It is persuaded.
