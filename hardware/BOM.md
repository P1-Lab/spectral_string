# Bill of Materials (BOM)

## 1. Structural Frame

- Maple beam (primary body)
- optional ash or carbon composite variant
- stainless steel mounting rails

---

## 2. String System

- phosphor bronze strings (standard set)
- stainless steel strings (high stability mode)
- hybrid coated steel (low-noise articulation)

---

## 3. Bridge Assembly

- hardened steel bridge anchor
- micro-adjust tail tension coupling
- non-resonant mounting interface

---

## 4. Lever System

- detented mechanical switch (2-position minimum)
- hardened pivot axle
- vibration-isolated mount

---

## 5. Sensors

- capacitive linear strip sensor
- force-sensitive resistors (bridge region)
- MEMS accelerometer (optional)

---

## 6. Hardware Isolation

- rubberized isolation mounts (non-compliant tuning zones)
- mechanical decoupling layers between sensor and structure

---

## 7. Fasteners

- stainless steel machine screws (M2–M4 range)
- non-loosening lock washers
- torque-rated assembly points

---

## 8. Electrical (Non-critical path)

- high impedance buffer stage (piezo front-end)
- shielded analog routing
- isolated ground plane architecture

---

## 9. Prohibited Materials

- plastic structural beams
- low-mass hollow resonant frames
- flexible PCB as structural element
