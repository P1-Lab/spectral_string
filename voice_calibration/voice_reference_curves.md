# Voice Reference Curves

Each VoiceState must converge to a deterministic physical response curve.

---

## ORCHESTRAL CORE
- flat spectral baseline
- controlled decay slope
- minimal harmonic drift

## DARK MASS
- low-frequency gain shift
- extended decay tail
- high inertia lag

## WOOD RESONANT
- midrange harmonic clustering
- asymmetric decay envelope

## SPLIT TRANSIENT
- fast attack spike
- steep decay curve

## METAL HYBRID
- extended high-frequency harmonic band
- reflective decay structure

## DRY STUDY
- flattened resonance curve
- minimal spectral bloom

## ROOM COUPLED
- external decay expansion into environment
- reduced internal damping signature
